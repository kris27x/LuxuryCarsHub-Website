Common Questions

Can customers book test drives of high-performance vehicles at SVehicules?

Yes, SVehicules offers test drives of high-performance vehicles. Our sales team can provide more information and help customers schedule a test drive that fits their schedule and preferences.

What kind of warranties or guarantees does SVehicules offer for its vehicles?

SVehicules offers warranties and guarantees for its vehicles, which vary depending on the vehicle's age and manufacturer. All vehicles undergo a thorough inspection process to ensure their quality and reliability. Additionally, SVehicules offers extended warranties and service contracts for additional coverage.

Does SVehicules offer any additional services, such as car detailing or customization?

Yes, SVehicules offers additional services such as car detailing and customization. Our expert technicians can provide a range of car detailing services to ensure that your high-performance vehicle always looks its best, including interior and exterior cleaning, paint correction, and ceramic coating. We also offer customization services to help you make your luxury car truly your own, with options such as bespoke paint colors, personalized interior finishes, and performance upgrades.

What kind of financing options are available for customers at SVehicules?

At SVehicules, we offer a range of financing options to help our customers purchase the high-performance vehicle of their dreams. We work with a variety of reputable lenders and financial institutions to provide competitive rates and flexible payment plans. Our financing options may include traditional car loans, leases, and other forms of financing that can be tailored to suit each customer's unique needs and budget. Our team of experienced finance specialists can help guide customers through the financing process and answer any questions they may have.

What kind of after-sales support does SVehicules provide to its customers?

SVehicules provides comprehensive after-sales support to its customers, including maintenance and repair services, warranty coverage, and customer service. Our dealership's team of expert technicians is dedicated to ensuring that every vehicle we sell is in top condition, and we offer regular maintenance services to help our customers keep their high-performance vehicles running smoothly. Additionally, we provide warranty coverage and support for any issues or concerns that may arise after the sale, and our customer service team is available to assist customers with any questions or issues they may have.

What kind of car maintenance and repair services does SVehicules offer?

SVehicules offers a range of car maintenance and repair services, including routine maintenance tasks such as oil changes, tire rotations, and brake inspections, as well as more extensive repairs and diagnostic services. Our team of highly skilled technicians uses state-of-the-art equipment to ensure that all vehicles are serviced and repaired to the highest standards of quality and safety. Additionally, we offer a variety of maintenance plans and warranties to help our customers keep their vehicles in top condition.

How does SVehicules ensure that its vehicles are in top condition before being sold to customers?

SVehicules employs a team of skilled technicians who carefully inspect and service each vehicle before it is offered for sale. All vehicles undergo a rigorous multi-point inspection to ensure that they meet SVehicules' high standards for performance, safety, and reliability. Any necessary repairs or maintenance are carried out using genuine parts and the latest diagnostic equipment to ensure that the vehicle is in top condition before being sold to customers.

How does SVehicules ensure that its customers are satisfied with their purchase?

SVehicules ensures that its customers are satisfied with their purchase by providing exceptional customer service and support throughout the car-buying process. This includes personalized assistance from knowledgeable sales professionals, the opportunity to test drive high-performance vehicles, and access to financing options that fit their needs and budget. Additionally, SVehicules offers warranties and guarantees for its vehicles, as well as after-sales support and maintenance services to ensure that customers are fully satisfied with their purchase long after they drive off the lot.
